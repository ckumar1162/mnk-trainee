The Best Anime According To Kaviyarasan

My Top Three Anime

Naruto

The journey of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with dreams of becoming the strongest leader (Hokage) of his village. Packed with emotional storytelling, incredible battles, and inspiring themes of perseverance and friendship.

Death Note

A psychological cat-and-mouse game between a genius high school student with a deadly notebook and a brilliant detective. Perfect if you love mind games and moral dilemmas.

Demon Slayer

A visually stunning anime following Tanjiro, a kind-hearted boy who becomes a demon slayer to avenge his family and save his sister. The animation and fight scenes are breathtaking.
